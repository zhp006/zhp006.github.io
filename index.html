Windows Task Reminder

2019 Spring

Memorizing material from the class is always a pain to most students, that's because most people don't know how to efficiently put knowledge into their brains. Luckily, there's this smart guy named Hermann Ebbinghaus, who discovered the secret of forgetting. I made this review schedule app according to his learning curve, aiming at helping students to overcome their fears of memorization.

CHEFS Web App

2018 Fall

CHEFS is a web search engine that can fetch a recipe from the database according to its users' requirements. This is a class project in my 3rd year of college. During the development process, I worked with 8 other talented individuals to solve every problem that we encountered. I designed the database schema for this culinary web app, and also took part in both the back-end and front-end development.
